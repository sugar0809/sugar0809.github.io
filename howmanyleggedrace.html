何人何脚!?/How Many Legged Races!?

「何人何脚!?」は、二人三脚の要領で足踏みをすると目の前のコンテンツが再生できるデジタルスポーツシステムです。
The “How Many Legged Race!?” is a variation of the three-legged race that can accommodate any number of participants as they synchronize their steps.



発表/Publications

Ayaka Sato, Anna Yokokubo, Itiro Siio, Jun Rekimoto, Collaborative Digital Sports Systems that Encourage Exercise, 16th International Conference on Human-Computer Interaction (HCII2014). [pdf]
